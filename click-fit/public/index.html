Our Story

Born in a Garage.
Built for Champions.

ClickFit started in 2012 with one coach, a borrowed barbell, and the belief that serious training shouldn't cost a fortune or require a fancy membership card. We kept it simple and built from there.

Now 2,400 members train with us across four locations. The coaches are hands-on, the classes are programmed to actually get results, and nobody here is just going through the motions.

Our Philosophy

“Put the work in and the results follow. That's the only thing we've ever told people; and it keeps being true.”
